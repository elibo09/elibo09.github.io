Details

Pharaoh's Curse is a pretended videogame for which I am developing and designing its art.
At the moment I have designed the main characters, an environment using Matte Painting, an UI and some poster-images that could work as the cover of the game.

The next step is turning all this 2D concepts into 3D and introduce them into Unity, and give them a professional and realistic look. Right now, I am working with the placeholder of the main character in Unity, but is still far away from its final look. So stay tuned for updates.

Since it is a big project, I invite you to check the documentation to get to know all the proccess and details better, along with the story of the game.

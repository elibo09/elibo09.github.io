Details

Welcome to Pirate Town! This 3D project is mainly focused in exploring and working with many different ways of animating.
Let's talk about our little pirate first... It is entirely modeled in 3ds Max with a very round, cartoon style and a lot of personality. It has later been rigged in 3ds Max and because of his caricatured shape an exhaustive skinning proccess adjusting the weights has been necessary. Once it was properly functioning, several animation layers where applied.
But that's not all. In order to bring the character alive, letting him talk or show hace expressions a morpher modifier has been applied and after modeling the different phonemes, controllers have been assigned to each one of them.

About the environment, it is very basic if we talk about modeling, with some low-poly assets made in 3ds Max. Once again the highlights are the different animations such us Noise for the water, Crowd Behaviour and Link Constraint for the barrels, Attachment Constraints for every floating object, Cloth modifiers for the flags, FFD4 modifier to move the parrots wings which movement has been captured with SnapShot turnt into morphers and later programmed with a Maxscript or even a Float Expression in the Transform Rotation of the wind vane.

Since it is a big project, I invite you to check the documentation to get to know all the proccess and details better.
